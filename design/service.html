ERP/CRM

ERP stands for Enterprise resource planning. ERP helps an organization to use a system of integrated applications to manage the business and automate many backend technical functions related to various aspects of technology and human resources.

In today’s word a firm cannot withstand without the latest technology in order to meet the needs of the ever changing and growing competitive market. ERP system acts as a single database system as it centralizes the entire database in one place and removes the hindrance of synchronizing changes between multiple systems.

ERP consists of Customer relations, Supply chain, Inventory, Purchasing, Human Resources, Finance, Sales, Manufacturing and Engineering. ERP immensely helps the management and employees to understand and improve the business performance.
